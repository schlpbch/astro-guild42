## Speaker

[Richard Stallman](https://www.stallman.org/) is the founder of the Free Software Movement and a prominent advocate for software freedom. He has been a key figure in the development of free software and has influenced the technology landscape significantly through his work on the GNU Project and the Free Software Foundation. See more about his work and contributions on his [serious biography](https://stallman.org/biographies.html#serious).
