## Speaker

We are proud to present [Gregor Hohpe](https://www.linkedin.com/in/ghohpe/), the world-famous elevator operating architect. He is the author of the best-selling book "Enterprise Integration Patterns", "Cloud Strategy", "Platform Strategy" and "The Software Architect Elevator". As a thought leader in the field of software architecture for over two decades he has been sharing his insights and experiences through his books, talks, and workshops. He is known for his ability to distill complex architectural concepts into practical advice that can be applied in real-world scenarios.

He worked at several IT consulting firms before joining Google as a developer in 2005. From 2012, he held multiple roles as an IT architect at Allianz Insurance. Five years later, he became Technical Director in Google's CTO office. In 2019, the Government of Singapore appointed Hohpe as an advisor for their Smart Nation initiative. From 2020 to 2024, he worked at Amazon's cloud subsidiary AWS, most recently in the role of Senior Principal Evangelist. Now, he is a freelance consultant and speaker. Additionally, he is a member of the IEEE Software Advisory Board.
