### Speaker
[Gregor Hohpe](https://www.linkedin.com/in/ghohpe/) the well known architect!
He worked at several IT consulting firms before joining Google as a developer in 2005. From 2012, he held multiple roles as an IT architect at Allianz Insurance. Five years later, he became Technical Director in Google's CTO office. In 2019, the Government of Singapore appointed Hohpe as an advisor for their Smart Nation initiative. From 2020 to 2024, he worked at Amazon's cloud subsidiary AWS, most recently in the role of Senior Principal Evangelist.

He is a member of the IEEE Software Advisory Board.
